ABOUT POP TELECOM

POP Telecom

About Us

POP Telecom  was incorporated in 2011, and is part of a group of companies that have been trading for over 25 years and have a well established & experienced team to ensure the service you receive is second to none!

We are one of the fastest growing telecommunication companies in the UK today, with an ethos of Simply Communicating by Communicating Simply. Our aim is to make the journey of joining and benefiting from our landline, broadband and mobile owned networks simple and clear.

We pride ourselves on delivering, Market Leading Consumer and Business tariffs backed up by our UK based Customer Service staff, trained to the highest standard, and committed to making sure your experience with POP Telecom will not be beaten.


Customer Service

Our customer service team arealways on hand to answer your calls or questions and offer any support and advice required. We are committed to making your experience with POP Telecom unique with old fashioned values. Call one of our team today on 0343 538 6611.


Our Network

We offer a strong, reliable home, business and Broadband network covering 96% of the UK Population, simply the largest fixed line & Broadband network in the UK. Our Mobile network covers E.E & VODAFONE offering flexible business contracts and aggressive Consumer deals covering 98% of the UK Population.
